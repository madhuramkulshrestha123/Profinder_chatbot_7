    Who is ProFinder?

    ProFinder is designed to simplify your shopping experience by addressing key challenges:
    • Pricing Trends: Get clear guidance on whether to buy now or wait for a better deal.
    • Price Comparisons: Compare prices across major e-commerce platforms in one place.
    • Optimal Purchase Timing: Receive recommendations on the best time to make a purchase based on historical data and upcoming sales.
    Our chatbot provides actionable insights and recommendations to help you shop smarter.
